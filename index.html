The Hunter's Dream

This page provides a brief overview of what hunting is and how you can get started with a hunting license in Maryland.

What is Hunting?

Hunting is the practice of pursuing, capturing, or killing wildlife, typically for food, recreation, or population control. It has been a part of human tradition for thousands of years and is often regulated to promote conservation and ecological balance.

Getting a Hunting License in Maryland

To hunt in Maryland, you must purchase a hunting license from the Maryland Department of Natural Resources (DNR). You can apply online through the DNR website or visit a licensed agent. New hunters must complete a hunter safety education course. Additional permits may be needed for specific game or seasons.
